Common Questions

What is EventEnergies ?

EventEnergies is a one-stop platform dedicated to ticketing of events, technology & trade fairs. EventEnergies lists comprehensive information about concerts, tours, plays, trade fairs, sporting events, seminars, conferences, training and workshops enabling event discovery and ticketing.

It connects organizers and attendees seamlessly, helping organizers list, promote and sell tickets all at one place and reach out to their target audience on an easy to use digital platform. We are a proud "Go-Green" initiative and we seek to make the entire event process paperless.

What events are published / listed on EventEnergies ?

EventEnergies lists information about concerts, plays, trade fairs, sports, seminars, conferences, spiritual events, corporate training, workshops, and the entire gamut of events. It is the go-to portal to be in the loop about what's happening, where and book tickets instantly.

How much does it cost to post an event ?

The process of event listing is completely free.

How many cities is EventEnergies available in ?

EventEnergies lists events all over India and is aggressively working towards an international presence.

EventEnergies is a unique platform dedicated entirely to events and trade-fairs industry. It attracts a large number of unique visitors with high conversion rate due to effortless online ticketing; there are also innovative promotional strategies to ensure visibility on the website and across social platforms along with uniquely tailored content with embedded call to action to boost ticket sales.

Attendees Questions

What can EventEnergies do for an Event ?

EventEnergies allows an organizer to include all the information about an event in an easy listing. The organizer can choose from different promotional plans to enhance visibility. It also increases conversion ratio as the user audience is highly targeted and hyper-local.

How secure is the information I subscribe to on EventEnergies ?

EventEnergies connects attendees and organizers seamlessly. All events can be accessed with one click. EventEnergies assigns a unique username and password for the attendees and organizers. Signing up, email alerts on important events are made simple and easy. Printing of ticket, Printing of event pass and subscribing to our newsletters have been simplified. Information about discounts from event organizers to attendees is accessible in one click.

What happens if an Event is cancelled ?

In case of cancellation by the event organizer EventEnergies will update it on the event listing and a personal mail will be sent to you. If the event that you registered for is a paid event you will also be notified about the fee refund via email. However cancellation of registration for an event that you have registered for, will vary and depend on the Organizer's terms & conditions.

Where do I find information about the event organizer ?

Every event that is listed on EventEnergies is curated and verified and has all the contact information about the organizer on the event listing. The information is available on the events page below the event details. To get the e-mail ID of organizer click on the name of the organizer and all the information you require is listed.

Do attendees need to pay a fee to register ?

An attendee is required to pay a fee in case of a paid event. The registration fee can be paid on EventEnergies using multiple payment options including e-wallets such as RazorPay and Paytm.
